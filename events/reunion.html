Every year we celebrate milestone anniversaries with the alumni, bringing them back to the College to share in the celebrations of our graduating seniors and tour the campus with student guides. We cap off the weekend with a gala reunion dinner.

2017

The 2017 anniversary reunion will begin at the college on the morning of FRIDAY, JUNE 2, 2017 when we will march in the Commencement processional, witness the ceremonies, have lunch and tour the campus.

On Sunday evening, June 4, 2017 there will be a reception and dinner at the Harmonie Club on 4 East 60th.

Speakers are to be announced.

Tickets are $190

You can register online or call us at 212-650-6525.

In honor of their anniversaries, the alumni of City College have raised hundreds of thousands of dollars for the College and our students.

Read about the Class Gift for your graduation year. You can print this pledge form and mail it with your gift OR make a gift online — just specify the Class Year! All gifts received by May 19, 2017 will be acknowledged in the reunion dinner program.

About

The City College Fund is a tax-exempt 501c(3) non-profit fundraising organization, established in 1946 to support the City College of New York in its mission to provide access and excellence in higher education, particularly to students who might not otherwise be able to afford to go to college. Gifts to The City College Fund directly benefit City College's extraordinary students,renowned faculty, innovative programs and schools.
