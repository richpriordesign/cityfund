The City College Fund provides our donors with the opportunity to create named scholarships awards, professorships, lectures and other special funds.

These gifts may be designated as endowments that generate interest for use in perpetuity, or they may be used directly to support the donor's chosen endeavor. Criteria for the disbursement of these special funds may be determined by the donor.

Scholarships and Awards, Lectures and Professorships may be named for a donor, in honor of or in memory of someone the donor wishes to recognize, or they may be given anonymously.

If you are interested in establishing a new fund at City College, please contact City College Fund Executive Director Elena Sturman.

To support an already existing fund, or to support the Annual Fund, please call us at 212-650-6525.
