Support

When Bob Adler '58 learned that baseball at CCNY had been discontinued due to budget cuts, he made a financial commitment to underwrite the program and raise funds from alumni and friends. He was honored for his efforts by the Alumni Varsity Association at their Hall of Fame Dinner in 2006.

Reconnected to the College, Bob joined the board of The City College Fund. Reconnected to the College, Bob joined the board of The City College Fund. He has since created a scholarship for the renewed ROTC program, supported Theatre on campus, contributed to the Jewish Studies program and lectured in the Economics Department on business and ethics and other topics. He also acts as a recruiter for baseball and basketball recruits-extolling the virtues of our great institution to high school candidates.

About

The City College Fund is a tax-exempt 501c(3) non-profit fundraising organization, established in 1946 to support the City College of New York in its mission to provide access and excellence in higher education, particularly to students who might not otherwise be able to afford to go to college. Gifts to The City College Fund directly benefit City College's extraordinary students,renowned faculty, innovative programs and schools.
