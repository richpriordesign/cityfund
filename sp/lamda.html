Lamda

An incredible adventure at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Arts.

Support

Education is the kindling of a flame, In the summers of 2013 and 2014, The City College Fund, through the generosity of alumna Pat Hill ‘76, partnered with the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art in London to provide a once-in-a-lifetime experience for some of CCNY's most talented performing arts majors.

(L-R) Rachel Baccus, Darilyn Castillo and Maggie Lalley – 2013 LAMDA recipients

“It was an incredible adventure to study Shakespearean acting at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Arts. To say that it was life-changing does not come close to expressing what this experience has meant for me.

Since returning to New York City and my classes at City College, I have striven to carry what I encountered on the mountain top down into the trenches. So far, I am pretty excited about the results. I have always loved all of my classes and activities at City College, and now I feel that I can truly engage with them at such a greater depth than I was capable of before. Many students are working to help put themselves through school or support their families or both.

What is common to every student at City College is that we all understand what a gift this education is because we are all so invested in it ourselves. Many students are working to help put themselves through school or support their families or both. Many are the first in their families to graduate from college. City College has given me a second chance at life- a second chance to pursue my passion and create the life for myself that I always imagined.” —Rachel Foote Baccus

About

The City College Fund is a tax-exempt 501c(3) non-profit fundraising organization, established in 1946 to support the City College of New York in its mission to provide access and excellence in higher education, particularly to students who might not otherwise be able to afford to go to college. Gifts to The City College Fund directly benefit City College's extraordinary students,renowned faculty, innovative programs and schools.
