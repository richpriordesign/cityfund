““As President of the Board of Directors of The City College Fund, I have the distinct pleasure of working with an impressive group of alumni volunteers who serve alongside me on The City College Fund Board of Directors. These are accomplished professionals who selflessly donate their time, money and talents to ensure that The City College Fund is governed to high fiduciary standards and is able to effectively meet the needs of the College and its students.

I am personally inspired by the hard work and dedication of not only my fellow Board members, but by the dynamic leadership of our Executive Director Elena Sturman and the ongoing commitment of City College President Lisa S. Coico. Working together to fulfill the mission of The City College Fund, we have made a difference in the lives of countless deserving students and have positively impacted the College.

I am very proud of the CCF's recent achievements and grateful for the support we have received from CCNY alumni, friends, corporations and foundations. It is only through the generosity of others that The City College Fund is able to help enrich City College and assist CCNY scholars.” — Steve Karafiol '71 President, The City College Fund
